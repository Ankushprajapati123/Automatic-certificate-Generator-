About Us

A fit mind is an active mind. Therefore, along with academic pursuits, students should also pay equal attention to games and sports. We believe that sports play a crucial role in the holistic development of a student's personality since it teaches an individual to imbibe sportsman spirit, to be humble in victory and gracious in defeat, and a spirit of co-operation, discipline, honesty, and integrity. The university provides all types of sports facilities for both indoor and outdoor games. Regular play is a vital part of the curriculum. The university has 20+ acres of playground. AKS University Students participated in the Satna Half Marathon organized on 3rd September by Pt. Ganesh Prasad Mishra Seva Nyas.
